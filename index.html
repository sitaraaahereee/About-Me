a little about me 🌷

a hyderabadi soul trying to find her aesthetic in the chaos of mumbai.

Hi! I'm Sitara, 17, an art lover with permanently paint-stained hands and a sketchbook that goes everywhere with me.

Born in Africa, currently figuring out Mumbai. Still a Hyderabadi at heart — the biryani is irreplaceable and so is the attitude. 🫀

I believe in late-night conversations, art that makes your chest tight, cats who pretend they don't love you, and yes — a pinch of salt in my coffee. Try it before you judge. ☕🧂
